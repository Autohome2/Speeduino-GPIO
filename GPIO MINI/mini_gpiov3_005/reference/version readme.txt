GPIO MINI V0.001
This firmware supports up to 16 output channels , 16 digital input channels and 16 analog channels from local sources subject to MCU capability. 
MCU supported are currently Arduino Mega2560. an stm32 version is under development and some evidence of this can be seen in the code. 
The  16 Output channels can be activated via one condition with logical operations of < , > and = , only onboard(local) io are supported in this version.
